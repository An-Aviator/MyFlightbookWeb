Each member of a club can do things based on their roles.

Every member has one of these mutually-exclusive roles:

  • Member - Regular club member. Can use the club
  • Admin - Club admin; all member privileges, but can also manage the club.
  • Owner - Club owner; all admin privileges, but can also delete the club. A club can only have one owner

Additionally, zero or more members can have additional roles (which are not mutually exclusive):

  • Maintenance Officer - Receives a monthly rollup flying report, including things like usage and oil added, and status of aircraft inspections
  • Treasurer - Receives a monthly rollup of flying, to aid in any billing.
  • Insurance Officer - Receives a monthly report of member flying, useful for informing insurance applications and renewals
